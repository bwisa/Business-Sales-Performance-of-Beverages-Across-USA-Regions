# Business-Sales-Performance-of-Beverages-Across-USA-Regions
Company Sales Performance of Beverages Across USA Regions, data cleaning, analysis and visualisation

# Problem Statement:
Understanding sales trends, regional performance, and product contribution to improve revenue.

# Conclusion:
Sales show strong end-year growth, with key regions and top performers driving revenue.

# Executive Summary

## Key Findings:
1. Strong revenue growth in Q4.
2. North region leads in sales contribution.
3. Baked goods and beverages dominate sales.

## Recommendations:
- Strengthen underperforming regions.
- Support mid-level sales staff.
- Increase high-value transactions.

# Business Problem & Decisions

## Business Problem:
Uneven sales distribution across regions and staff.

## Decisions Supported:
- Resource allocation by region
- Sales team performance improvement
- Product focus and expansion

# Data Approach & Methodology

## Data Source: Kaggle
Tools: Excel, Pivot Tables

### Steps:
- Created pivot tables for revenue by salesperson
- Sorted sales performance
- Analyzed category percentages
- Compared sales reps performance
- Cleaned data (removed blanks)

# Key Findings
- Revenue peaks in June and December
- Top 3 salespeople contribute majority of revenue
- Majority sales are low-value transactions
- Balanced but slightly North-dominant regional sales
